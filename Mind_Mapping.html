Follow Your Mind's Map

 When you spend all your energy trying to stay on top of new ideas, you'll only be disappointed when they don't work well with your website and marketing. Don't fall victim to the “fad-feeding” technique. Just because the Web changes much faster than traditional media, remember that you can't succeed without the basic foundation of planning and testing.

 Mind mapping allows you to visually organize your ideas and clarify your thinking.It's the Swiss Army Knife of planning. The process is similar to how your brain creates ideas, so it’s easy to pick up and use immediately.

 You have two choices: paper and pen or software. You can map out your thoughts with a colored pencil and paper, or you can upgrade to efficient software. The best part about the latter is the ability to move things around and quickly refine ideas. I personally use MindManager from Mindjet because it functions well with MS Office and other applications. Though, I’m confident that with some research you’ll be able to find what works best for yourself.

 

Immediate benefits on mind-mapping:


  You see the “big picture” and details simultaneously

  Encourages creative input

  Helps you both create and reach your goals

  Allows you to create new products and manage content efficiently

  Gives you a new perspective outside the traditional hierarchical outline


As soon as you have your “big picture” mapped out, you can start to focus on drilling through the sections. You will see new ideas jump out at new when you organize your business in this way, making it simple to analyze where you are now. That’s why I use them to develop new websites and redesign current ones.
